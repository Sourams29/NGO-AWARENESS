About InAmigos Foundation

A registered NGO transforming lives through compassion, community, and commitment since 2020.

Who We Are

More Than an NGO — A Movement

InAmigos Foundation was founded in 2020 with one simple belief: change begins when people come together. Since then, we have grown into one of India's most dynamic youth-led NGOs, working across education, environment, animal welfare, women empowerment, and social development.

Our name "InAmigos" — derived from "In Friends" — reflects our core philosophy: that friendship and solidarity are the greatest tools for change. We operate through a vast network of passionate volunteers, interns, and change-makers across India.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

🔭

Our Vision

To create a compassionate, equitable, and sustainable India where every individual — regardless of background — has access to education, dignity, and opportunity. We envision an India where no child goes uneducated, no animal is left uncared for, and no tree unplanted.

🎯

Our Mission

To mobilize India's youth as agents of social change through structured volunteer programs, internship opportunities, and community-driven projects. We aim to bridge the gap between privilege and need — one initiative, one volunteer, one life at a time.

Our Journey

Growing Stronger Every Year

2020

Foundation Established

InAmigos Foundation was established as a Section 8 company during the COVID-19 pandemic, initially focused on food distribution and community support.

2021

80G & 12A Certification

Received 80G and 12A certifications, allowing tax benefits for donors. Launched Project BACHPANSHALA with 50+ volunteer educators.

2022

NITI Aayog Registration

Registered on NITI Aayog's Darpan portal. Expanded to 10+ cities. Launched Project JEEV for animal welfare and Project PRAKRITI for environment.

2023

CSR-1 Registration & Scale

Achieved CSR-1 registration enabling corporate partnerships. Crossed 20,000+ volunteers trained and 10,000+ beneficiaries impacted.

2024

Pan-India Expansion

Active in 25+ cities, 50,000+ beneficiaries, 20,000+ trees planted, 30,000+ interns trained. Launched Project UDAAN and Project VIKAS.
