Transforming Lives One Project at a Time

#ProjectSEVA

Project SEVA

Project SEVA is InAmigos Foundation's flagship food distribution and care initiative. We run regular food drives, distribute essentials to underprivileged families, and provide support to the homeless. SEVA operates across 20+ cities and has served over 50,000 meals.

50,000+ Meals Served Volunteer
#ProjectBACHPANSHALA

Project BACHPANSHALA

BACHPANSHALA means "school of childhood." This project provides free education, learning materials, and mentorship to underprivileged children aged 5–15. Our 200+ volunteer educators run weekend sessions in slums, rural areas, and shelter homes.

8,000+ Children Educated Volunteer
#ProjectJEEV

Project JEEV

Project JEEV is our animal welfare initiative. We rescue injured and abandoned animals, run regular feeding drives for street dogs and cats, organize veterinary camps, and spread awareness about responsible pet ownership. JEEV stands for "living being."

5,000+ Animals Helped Volunteer
#ProjectUDAAN

Project UDAAN

UDAAN means "flight" — and this project is about helping India's youth soar. We provide internship opportunities, skill development workshops, career guidance, and mentorship to young graduates and students from underprivileged backgrounds.

15,000+ Interns Placed Volunteer
#ProjectPRAKRITI

Project PRAKRITI

PRAKRITI (meaning "nature") is our environmental conservation initiative. We organize tree plantation drives, clean-up campaigns, plastic-free awareness programs, and environmental education workshops. 20,000+ trees planted and counting!

20,000+ Trees Planted Volunteer
#ProjectVIKAS

Project VIKAS

VIKAS means "development." This project focuses on women empowerment — providing vocational training, digital literacy, financial education, and self-defense workshops to women from marginalized communities. VIKAS is building India one woman at a time.

3,000+ Women Empowered Volunteer
