Our Mission

Creating Real Change Across India

Founded in 2020, InAmigos Foundation is a registered NGO working tirelessly to uplift communities — one initiative at a time.

Children Education

Project BACHPANSHALA brings quality education to underprivileged children through our volunteer-driven initiative.

Environment Conservation

Project PRAKRITI drives tree plantations and awareness campaigns across urban and rural India.

Animal Welfare

Project JEEV rescues and rehabilitates animals, running feeding drives and veterinary camps.

Food Distribution

Project SEVA ensures no one goes hungry — we run regular food drives for the underprivileged.
